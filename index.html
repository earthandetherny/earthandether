Our Philosophy

Earth + Ether is a destination for time-worn, soulful objects — an intentionally curated selection of vintage and antique treasures from around the world.

Each piece is hand-selected to fit our bohemian traveler ethos: a deep reverence for globally-inspired designs, the enduring beauty of natural materials, and the unique history embedded in every piece.
